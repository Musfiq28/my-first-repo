Bandarban

Bandarban is a small town in Chittagong Division, Bangladesh. It is the headquarters of Bandarban District. It is located on the Sangu River. Some of the popular tourist attractions in Bandarban include the golden temple, Boga lake, Nilgiri hill, Chimbuk hill, and Sangu river, among others. The district also offers opportunities for trekking, hiking, and camping, making it a favorite destination for adventure enthusiasts.

Chittagong

Chittagong s the second-largest city in Bangladesh.Home to the Port of Chittagong, it is the busiest port in Bangladesh and the Bay of Bengal.he city is also known as business capital of Bangladesh. It is the administrative seat of an eponymous division and district. The city is located on the banks of the Karnaphuli River between the Chittagong Hill Tracts and the Bay of Bengal. The Greater Chittagong Area had a population of more than 140 million in 2022. In 2022, the Chittagong District had a population of approx 9.2 Million according to a census conducted by Government of BangladeshIn 2022, the city area had a population of more than 5.6 million.The city is home to many large local businesses and plays an important role in the Bangladeshi economy.
